GAMEPLAY TIPS:

• START: Begin at Frozen Gate (entrance) with basic equipment
• PROGRESSION: Explore south-to-north, west-to-east for difficulty progression
• KEY BATTLES:
  - Ice Troll (Crystal Hall) - First major obstacle
  - Frozen Guardian (Glacier Depths) - Mid-game challenge
  - Glacier Golem (Frost Throne) - Final boss encounter
• BEST LOOT: Treasure Vault (requires solving fire riddle for Frozen Crown)
• HEALING: Multiple healing potions scattered throughout, solve echo riddle for extra
• DEAD END: Ice Bridge is a dead-end with healing potion (risk vs reward)
